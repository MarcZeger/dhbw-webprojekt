Foot2Afrika

Foot2Afrika is a Tanzanian community based non-governmental organization that works to create local solutions to poverty. We work with villages, individuals, and other organizations to improve access to education, healthcare, food and water, especially for women and children. Our local collaborative approach to development makes us unique.

Our newest and most comprehensive project to date, the Moshi Youth Village , is a community education center that provides training in literacy, computer skills, arts, trades and agriculture to children and adults in the rural communities adjacent to Moshi, Tanzania.

our work

We work in the community to help women, children, and conservation in the Kilimanjaro area of Tanzania.

our story

Foot2Afrika is run by passionate local and international volunteers, whose vision is to create a better Tanzania.
